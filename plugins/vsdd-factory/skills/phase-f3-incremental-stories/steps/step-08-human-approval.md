# Step 8: Human Approval Gate

Present new stories to the human for review and approval.

## Inputs

- New story specs
- Extended dependency graph
- Conflict report (if any)
- Effort estimate and critical path

## Actions

1. Present to the human:
   - New stories with acceptance criteria
   - Extended dependency graph (visual)
   - Conflict report (if any)
   - Effort estimate and critical path
2. Wait for explicit human approval

## Outputs

- Human approval (explicit "approved" or revision request)

## Completion Criteria

- Human has explicitly approved the new stories
- Phase F3 is COMPLETE only when approval is granted
- If rejected: iterate on specific stories per human feedback
