Invariant confirmed:
  - emit_sink_error() sends directly to the internal event channel (tokio::mpsc::Sender)
  - This channel bypasses SinkRegistry::submit_all() entirely
  - No code path from emit_sink_error() can reach SinkRegistry
  - Therefore: a sink failure cannot trigger another sink failure through the registry
    (no recursive loop risk)
