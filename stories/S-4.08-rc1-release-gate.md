## Narrative
- **As a** release engineer
- **I want to** cut the `1.0.0-rc.1` tag after all gate criteria are met
- **So that** the final hardening sprint can begin against a stable feature set

has been stable for at least 2 weeks. Validates rc.1 gate criteria 8–14
from the design doc.
