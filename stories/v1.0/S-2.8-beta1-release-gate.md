then cuts the `1.0.0-beta.1` tag and ships to the marketplace. First

## Notes

This is the "release gate" pattern ŌĆö a story that's really a checklist
that the rest of the epic has to pass to ship. No new code lands here.

If a gate fails, the story stays open and the blocking story reopens
until resolved. The release-gate story doesn't "ship with known bugs";
that's what rc.N is for.

Post-release, `1.0.0-beta.2..N` iterations go through the same gates,
but with shorter scope (just the bug fixes, not a full walkthrough).
Document the shorter rerun procedure after the first cycle.

After beta ships, Tier E unblocks. S-3.x + S-4.x stories can all begin
in parallel.
