## Stretch-Anchor Disclosure

This story applies the **F-007 (Wave 3) / F-002 (Wave 5) / F-005 (Wave 6) sanctioned-template-anchor pattern**:

- **Story `subsystems[]`**: declares `SS-10` (target_module is `scripts/bump-version.sh` which lives under SS-10 implementing-module wildcards per ARCH-INDEX line 83).
- **Story `behavioral_contracts[]`**: anchors to `BC-9.01.001` BC (subsystem: SS-09 per BC frontmatter).
- **Pattern**: BC-subsystem (SS-09) != story.subsystems[] (SS-10). This is intentional — `scripts/bump-version.sh` is invoked by SS-10 as a CLI/release-tooling surface but its semantic contract (semver format) is owned by SS-09 BCs that codify the release-tooling discipline.
- **Cross-subsystem ACs**: AC-9 (`bump-version.sh 1.0.0` runs successfully) exercises BC-9.01.001 postcondition 1 directly. AC-8 (CHANGELOG monotonicity) exercises BC-9.01.001 postcondition 2. All other ACs are gate-aggregation criteria [process-gap] — uncontracted SS-10 behaviors. Note: AC-9 invokes `bump-version.sh 1.0.0` (stable format) — BC-9.01.001 H1 names "prerelease format" but its postcondition 1 ("Version bump succeeds") is implicitly broader. Per Waves 5+6 (S-2.06 / S-2.04 patterns), BC-9.01.001 postcondition 1 is implicitly broader. v1.1 BC candidate (BC-10.13.011 or new) could explicit stable-format scope.
- **Codification path**: 11 v1.1 BC candidates (BC-10.13.001..011) registered for currently-uncontracted SS-10 behaviors (Release.yml prerelease flag, stable-tag false branch, prerelease banner, GH "latest" semantics, gate-dependency-closure, shakedown-stability, documentation completeness, SDK-publish gate, marketplace default switch). When SS-10 BC backfill closes, primary anchors will move SS-10 → SS-10 BCs.
